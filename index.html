About Me

Hello! My name is Jordan Pettiford and I'm a student journalist and aspiring filmmaker based in New York and Atlanta. Currently, I'm a rising junior at Columbia University double majoring in Film and Media Studies and Creative Writing. Outside of class, you can find me working on my fiction and nonfiction writing, exploring the city, and searching for stories for the Columbia Daily Spectator, the independent student newspaper where I serve as video editor. After first becoming interested in video journalism while making short fiction films with friends in Fulton County Schools' Audio, Video, Technology and Film program, my more recent work has been focused on nonfiction and includes a music journalism segment inspired by NPR's tiny dorm concert and an archival mini-doc on the nation's first LGBT student organization. Through the years I've gained experience taking videos from pre to post production; whether it's research, scripting, videography, or editing in Premiere Pro or Final Cut X, I'd love to produce or help out with whatever your project may be!
